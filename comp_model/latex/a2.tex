\section{3.2: Steady-state Population Model}

We will let $u_j < 1$ for all $j$. We also let the following be true:

\begin{equation*}
  \sum_j \beta_j \Big(\prod_{k<j}(1-u_k) \Big) = 1\\
\end{equation*}

Consider the following matrix:

\begin{equation}
  P = 
   \begin{pmatrix}
     \beta_1 & \beta_2 & ... & \beta_{N-1} & \beta_N\\
     1-u_1 & & & &\\
     & 1-u_2 & & &\\
     & & ... & &\\
     & & 1-u_{N-1} & &\\
     & & & 1-u_{N} & 0\\
   \end{pmatrix}
\end{equation}
